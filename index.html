Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my printer offline?

Usually due to connection loss, wrong default printer setting, or spooler errors.

Do I need new drivers?

If your printer stopped working after update, reinstall latest official drivers.

Why prints are faded?

Low ink, clogged heads, or draft mode can cause faded prints.
